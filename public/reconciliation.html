How to import into Zoho Books (3 steps):
1. Go to Zoho Books → Invoices
2. Click ⋮ More → Import Payments → upload zoho_payments_import.csv
3. Map fields: Invoice Number → Invoice Number · Amount Received → Amount · Bank Charges → Bank Charges · Payment Date → Payment Date

What Bank Charges means: This is Pathao's fee (COD % + delivery charge). Zoho records it as an expense and closes the invoice as fully paid. ✅

⚠️ Partial Orders: Customer received some items, returned others. Run each through the Partial Order Fixer tool to check Nuport, then paste instructions to Claude to fix the invoice and record payment.

⚠️ Partial Orders: Some items delivered, some returned. Run each through the Partial Order Fixer to check Nuport, then paste instructions to Claude to fix the invoice and record the correct payment.

💰 Return Income orders: Product returned, customer paid delivery charge, you profited at least ৳1. Record in Zoho Books → Banking → City Bank → Money In → Other Income with the net profit amount.

❌ Failed Delivery orders: Customer refused / returned without paying. Pathao deducted this from your settlement. Record in Zoho Books → Banking → City Bank → Money Out → Expense → Delivery Expense with the loss amount.

Net Settlement (Bank) = the exact amount Pathao deposited to your City Bank account.
